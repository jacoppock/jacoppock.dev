The EHR AI Assistant is a cutting-edge project aimed at revolutionizing healthcare by integrating artificial intelligence into Electronic Health Record systems. This assistant is designed to streamline clinical workflows, provide intelligent insights, and ultimately improve patient care.

Key features:
- Natural language processing for efficient data entry and retrieval
- Predictive analytics for patient risk assessment
- Automated coding suggestions for accurate billing
- Intelligent summarization of patient histories

This project showcases the potential of AI in healthcare, particularly in enhancing the usability and effectiveness of EHR systems.
